Problem Statement

“Reminders” is an app that is meant to help users stay organized. However, the current iteration lacks personalization/incentivization features and doesn’t account for–or celebrate– the progress that a user is making.

Solution

User research led our team to several modifications such as changing reminders to goals, users creating action steps to work towards for each goal and ways to keep the user motivated to complete their goal through rewards.

Objectives & Goals

We are looking to create a positive experience, not a nagging experience. When users have a positive experience– when they are reminded of the reasons they are working towards a “goal” and to reward themselves– we aren’t just building healthy habits but self-esteem. That is the true goal– not to complete tasks but to use those completed tasks as a reason to feel good about oneself. What we’re building isn’t just healthier habits, but the self esteem that comes from those healthier habits.
